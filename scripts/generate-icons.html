Generate PWA Icons from SVG

Instructions:

  1. Open public/favicon.svg in your image editor (GIMP, Photoshop, Figma)
  2. Export as PNG with these sizes:
    • 192x192 → save as public/icon-192.png
    • 512x512 → save as public/icon-512.png
  3. Or use online converter: https://cloudconvert.com/svg-to-png
